InterMine Training Portal

InterMine Training Portal is developed for users of InterMine, which integrates biological data sources, making it easy to query and analyse data.

Training Workshops

We typically carry out a few training workshops each year - keep your eye on our calendar or twitter feed to find out when registration is open.
